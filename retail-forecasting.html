Real-Time Retail Sales Forecasting & Inventory Optimization

View on GitHub

The Challenge

Brick-and-mortar stores and e-commerce platforms struggle with stockouts and overstock alike. Forecasts ran on stale data, forcing reactive restocking that either left shelves empty or piled up unsold inventory.

My Approach

  • Streaming Pipeline: Built a near real-time ETL workflow on AWS Redshift, ingesting daily sales, promotions, and web clickstream data via Python scripts.
  • Hybrid Forecasting: Deployed both ARIMA and Prophet models per SKU to capture seasonal trends, promotions impact, and sudden demand spikes.
  • Model Selection: Automated back-testing to choose the best model per category—achieving median accuracy across products.
  • Visualization: Created Tableau dashboards showing live forecast vs. actuals, highlighting low-coverage SKUs and alerting buyers.
  • Inventory Rules: Defined replenishment thresholds based on “forecast + safety stock,” reducing manual guesswork.

Results & Impact

  • 92% Forecast Accuracy across multiple product lines, outperforming legacy monthly methods by 15 percentage points.
  • 20% Reduction in Stockouts, leading to an uptick in on-shelf availability and a projected 5% revenue lift during peak season.
  • Inventory turns improved by 12%, freeing up 7% of working capital previously tied in excess stock.
